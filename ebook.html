Improving the eBook Experience

I got into a conversation with an acquaintance about the digital reading experience, which got me interested in thinking about eBooks from a user experience perspective. My goal in this project was to improve the experience of reading eBooks to more closely resemble the experience of reading a physical book.

User Research

I interviewed five people between the ages of 25 and 35. I asked them questions pertaining to their reading habits and preferences.

Here are some of the questions I asked.

  • How do you read books? (hard copy, ebook reader, app, audio, etc.)
  • How often do you read? What factors affect how often you read?
  • Why do you read?
  • What medium do you use the most often to read books? Why?
  • What is your favorite medium to read books on? Why?
  • Do you tend to buy books or borrow them? Why?
  • How do you find new books to read?
  • Have you ever used an eBook reader? What did you like about it? What did you dislike about it? How about reading online?

ABOUT

I'm currently a student at UC Berkeley majoring in cognitive science major. I'm mainly interested in the design of products, but also dabble in teaching computer science, and am interested in applications of code to other disciplines. Among other things, I am a casual photographer and Instagrammer. I also play cello, and belong to a group called Celli@Berkeley. In addition, I can read, write, and speak in Japanese. In my spare time, I like to read, swim, rock climb, cook, and write.


I am currently taking CogSci/Psych C126 (Perception), Comparative Literature R1B (Fictions of Science, Technology, and Society), and Japanese 100X (Advanced Japanese for Heritage Learners). I am also taking the {design} DeCal (student-run course focused on human-centered design).
